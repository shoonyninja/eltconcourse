Diagrams and charts

Many people respond well to visual representations of complex arrangements.  You may not.
On this page, you will find some of the diagrams and charts used in many guides in this site to summarise or illustrate concepts which are otherwise difficult to understand from the prose descriptions.
Much detail is lost, of course, as the diagrams are meant either as summaries or another way to visualise specific parts of guides.
These images are covered by the usual share-alike copyright obligations as everything else on this site.
