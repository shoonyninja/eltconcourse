The TKT Module 3 Revision Course

Do not do the revision course until you have finished the course for Module 3 on this site or taken a face-to-face preparation course.

This revision course covers all the topics in Module 3 of the TKT examination.

How to do the revision

You will need a pen and paper to respond to tasks and questions before checking your answers

In all the sections, you need to respond to the task or question by writing some short notes.
