Are you ready for Module 1?

The link below will take you to a short, multiple-choice test on the main ideas in Module 1 of the Teaching Knowledge Test.

Do the test and then make a note of your score at the end.

If you score above 15, you are probably ready to try the examination.  If you don't, you need to look at the test again and use the guides in this section of the site to learn more and extend your knowledge.
Don't spend your money on the examination yet.
