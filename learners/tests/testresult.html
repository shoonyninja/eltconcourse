Your test result

Please remember that this was only a short test of some of your English.  It is not very accurate!
The levels are those used in Europe and called the Common European Framework.

Your test score Your CEF level
0 to 18 A1
19 to 36 A2
37 to 55 B1
56 to 72 B2
73 to 90 C1
90+ C2
Very roughly
A1 is beginner to elementary
A2 is elementary to pre-intermediate
B1 is intermediate
B2 is upper intermediate
C1 is advanced
C2 is very advanced (at near native-speaker level)
Of course, some people are better in some areas than others.  You may be good at grammar but bad at speaking, have a good vocabulary but find writing difficult and so on.
So try lots of the lessons and exercises to find the right level for you.
