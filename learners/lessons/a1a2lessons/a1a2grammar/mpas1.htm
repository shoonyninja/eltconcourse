Middle position adverbs
Task 1: Look at the words in red here:

 

I usually walk the dog on Sundays.
I have nearly finished.
He will soon know.
I only wanted his email address.
She often arrives before 8.
They still haven't got enough money.
I really don't like this cheese.
I can't even remember his name.
Task 2: Can you put these adverbs into three groups?

Words that say how often - these are adverbs of frequency.
Words that say when - these are adverbs of time.
Words that say how much - these are adverbs of degree.
Do this on a piece of paper and then click here.
