ELT Concourse for learners of English

Welcome to the learning zone

This area contains short exercises in English as well as some longer lessons.

The levels are based on the Common European Framework.  Like this:


Understanding Tenses This is a guide with a set of tests to help you understand how English tenses work and what they mean.
A simple grammar of English This grammar covers only basic English grammar but it is a good place to start.  Go to this link to download your free copy.
Take a language test If you are not sure of your level, you can take the test first.  At the end of the test, write down your score before you click on 'Finish'.
Language lessons Short lessons in some areas of English grammar and vocabulary as well as reading and writing skills at three levels.
Language exercises Exercises in grammar, vocabulary, reading and listening at three levels.
Language analysis For advanced learners only!  This is a 10-unit course covering the elements of pronunciation, word class, content and function words, subjects and objects, tenses and aspects, modality, sentences, phrases, clauses and text structures.
If you hope one day to teach English, this may be for you.

If you have problems with some language areas, you can ask us a question.
