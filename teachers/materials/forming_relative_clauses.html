Forming relative pronoun clauses

This is a very short video presentation concerning how to make relative clauses with who and which.
If your learners are having problems, it may help.

This is sometimes a very troublesome area because many languages either do not have such things or make relative clauses in dramatically different ways.
This video presentation may help people understand how to make some of them but it will not help with meaning.

You can play it full screen by clicking on the control.

You can also download the video and save it to your computer by right-clicking this link.  Then it's easy to incorporate into a lesson of your choice.
