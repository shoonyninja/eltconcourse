The 7 meanings of over

looking over the sea

This is a troublesome preposition because it has seven different meanings and they rarely translate across languages.

This video presentation may help people understand it and use it correctly.

You can play it full screen by clicking on the control.

You can also download the video and save it to your computer by right-clicking this link.  Then it's easy to incorporate into a preposition lesson of your choice.
