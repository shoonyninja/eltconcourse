Links

ELT Concourse has erased the content of this page.  Sorry about that.

Briefly, the reasons are:

  1. Many links have been altered to redirect to commercially oriented websites and individuals and that runs counter to ELT Concourse's charter, of course.
  2. The fact that links were available from this site has encouraged some to claim that ELT Concourse approves of or endorses the content of the site.  It doesn't.

For help in finding useful links and resources (of which there are many) just use a search engine to check 'useful links for ELT'.

Good luck!
